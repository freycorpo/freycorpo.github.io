Product Description

Bundle Up And Bounce Back

Equip your family and yourself with the reliability of satellite connectivity. Bundle up and bounce back with the Iridium 9555 Satellite Phone Connected Not Stranded bundle with included 200 minutes valid for 6 Months. 

Disasters can happen anytime, anywhere, and it is important to anticipate risks and plan physical essentials as well as communications to stay safe. Iridium provides a critical lifeline during emergencies and calls on individuals in disaster-prone areas to be proactive. Iridium and its partners are well suited to equip end users with satellite technology, so they can communicate if a disaster occurs. Iridium’s global and reliable network, as well as rugged and powerful phone Iridium 9555, could help save a person’s life in a disaster. 

9555 Satellite Phone Connected Not Stranded Bundle Includes:

  • 200 Minutes of prepaid airtime (expiry period 6 months – Unused minutes are eligible to carry forward)
  • AC travel charger
  • International plug
  • Battery
  • Hands-free headset
  • Antenna adapter & portable auxiliary antenna
  • DC car charger
  • Leather holster
  • USB Cable (mini USB)
  • SIM Card

Iridium 9555 has all of the voice and data features you have come to expect from our trusted satellite phones — plus a few notable additions:

  • Up to 4 hours of talk time, 30 hours of standby
  • Improved SMS and email messaging capability
  • Integrated speakerphone
  • Upgraded mini-USB data port

Dimensions

  • Dimensions: 143 mm (L) x 55 mm (W) x 30 mm (D)
  • Weight: 266 g (9.4 oz)
  • Operating Temperature Range: -10 °C to 55 °C

Duration

  • Standby time: Up to 30 hours
  • Talk time: Up to 4 hours

Display

  • 200 character illuminated graphic display
  • Volume, signal and battery strength meters
  • Illuminated weather-resistant keypad

Calling Features

  • Integrated speakerphone
  • Quick-connect to Iridium voice mail
  • Two-way SMS and short email capability
  • Pre-programmable International Access Code (00 or )
  • Mailbox for voice, numeric & text messages
  • Selectable ring and alert tones (8 choices)

Memory

  • 100-entry internal address book, with capacity for multiple phone numbers, email addresses and notes
  • SIM card address book with 155-entry capacity
  • Call history retains received, missed and dialed calls
