Product Description

The Iridium GO! 6 Month Prepaid SIM Card provides global voice, text messaging and data access. There’s no activation fee and cards can be activated and refilled online. 

  • Data/Calls to another Go! Or Iridium handset: 400 minutes
  • Calls to cell phone or landline: 200 minutes
  • Sent Text Messages: 1200
  • Plan valid for 6 months from the time of purchase
