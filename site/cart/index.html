Frey Satellite Corporation specializes in satellite technology, including satellite phones and other Iridium hardware, satellite airtime plans, scientific instruments, and defence and security products. Frey Satellite Corporation is a renowned Value-Added Reseller (VAR) of Iridium® satellite products.
